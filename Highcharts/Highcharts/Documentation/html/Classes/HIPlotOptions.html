Overview

The plotOptions is a wrapper object for config objects for each seriestype. The config objects for each series can also be overridden foreach series item as given in the series array.Configuration options for the series are given in three levels. Optionsfor all series in a chart are given in the plotOptions.series object. Then options for all series of a specific type aregiven in the plotOptions of that type, for example plotOptions.line.Next, options for one single series are given in the series array.
